第二品·第十二经 · 万祇沙经

经集 · Sutta Nipata

← 第二品·第十一经 目录 Contents 第二品·第十三经 →

长老尼拘律（Nigrodhakappa）已入灭——对于那位出家的婆罗门，他已深入禅定——我内心产生了疑问，世尊：他是否已超越了死？ 【世尊】他确实已断除了渴爱，在法中——就像一股流水中的矢，他渡越了难以渡越的苦海。 【万祇沙】他以名为「智慧之眼」的眼——看见了一切诸行，了知了诸行之生与灭——他以究竟智，以人类之中罕见的解脱，而得解脱。 这就是我对尼拘律长老的理解——智慧之眼——名为「最终圆满解脱」的解脱，他以此而解脱。 啊，世尊！我向「圆满正觉的那位」顶礼，向「能看见」的那位顶礼——你以胜解安住于法中，以真实告诉了我。 那位崇高的牟尼所教导的——他具有一切智，以解脱一切缚束——如实知道法，了知法的意义，他了知法道。 【世尊】万祇沙，如你所赞叹的——你就是如此！是的，他已圆满解脱，断除了一切有漏，已超越了彼岸。

逐句解释

长老尼拘律已入灭——对于那位出家的婆罗门，他已深入禅定——我内心产生了疑问，世尊：他是否已超越了死？

万祇沙（Vaṅgīsa）是佛教传统中最著名的诗人比丘，以即兴诗偈著称。他的导师尼拘律（Nigrodhakappa）已经去世，万祇沙以诗偈向佛陀询问：导师是否已经真正解脱、超越了死亡？这个问题不是抽象的神学问题，而是一位弟子对已逝导师的深切关怀——他想知道，他所尊敬的人，是否真的抵达了修行的终点。

The Elder Nigrodhakappa has passed away — for that brahmin who went forth, deeply absorbed in meditation — a doubt has arisen in me, Blessed One: has he gone beyond death?

Vaṅgīsa was the most celebrated poet-monk of early Buddhism, renowned for composing spontaneous verse. His teacher Nigrodhakappa has died, and Vaṅgīsa's question is personal and urgent: did his teacher actually attain liberation, or did he die still bound? The question frames the entire sutta: it is a test of whether the practice works — whether a person who dedicated their life to the Dhamma truly arrives. The Buddha's confirmation, and Vaṅgīsa's subsequent praise, together constitute both an elegy and a declaration of faith in the path.

【世尊】他确实已断除了渴爱，在法中——就像一股流水中的矢，他渡越了难以渡越的苦海。

佛陀以两个比喻确认尼拘律的解脱。「断除了渴爱」（pahīnatanhā，渴爱已断）是阿罗汉果的标准描述：渴爱（taṇhā）是轮回的根本驱动力，断除渴爱即是解脱的根本。「就像一股流水中的矢」：箭矢射入水流，一路穿越，不被水流阻挡——这比喻尼拘律以精进穿越了苦海的洪流，没有被任何阻碍拦截。「渡越了难以渡越的苦海」是整个修行路径的隐喻性完成。

The Blessed One: He has indeed cut off craving, in the Dhamma — like an arrow in a current of water, he has crossed the flood of suffering, difficult to cross.

The Buddha confirms the attainment with two images. The cutting of taṇhā (craving) is the standard description of arahantship — craving is the engine of rebirth; its cessation is liberation. The arrow-in-water image is unusual: an arrow shot into a moving current continues along its trajectory, cutting through the flow without being diverted. Applied to Nigrodhakappa, it suggests that his practice drove through the stream of existence without deviation — straight to the other shore. The confirmation is direct and unambiguous, as befits an inquiry made by a grieving student.

【万祇沙】他以名为「智慧之眼」的眼——看见了一切诸行，了知了诸行之生与灭——他以究竟智，以人类之中罕见的解脱，而得解脱。

万祇沙以诗偈回应，以诗人的方式赞叹导师的解脱。「智慧之眼」（paññācakkhu，慧眼）是对洞见的比喻：以一种特殊的内在视觉，看见一切诸行（sabbe saṅkhārā，一切有为法）的生与灭。这是对无常观（aniccānupassanā）实践的诗意描述——持续地看见一切现象升起与消失，直到洞见深入到足以斩断执取的程度。「人类之中罕见」强调这种解脱的珍贵，也间接赞叹导师的成就。

Vaṅgīsa: With the eye called the eye of wisdom — seeing all conditioned things, knowing their arising and passing — he was liberated through final knowledge, through the rare liberation among humans.

Vaṅgīsa's response is characteristically poetic: he praises his teacher's attainment by naming its mechanism. The 'eye of wisdom' (paññācakkhu) is a recurring Pali metaphor for insight — an interior vision that sees what ordinary eyes cannot. What it sees is the arising and passing (udaya-vaya) of all conditioned phenomena — impermanence seen not as a concept but as a direct, repeated perception. This sustained seeing is the engine of liberation: when impermanence is genuinely perceived rather than merely known, clinging dissolves. The 'rareness' of this liberation among humans underscores how difficult the achievement is and how praiseworthy the teacher's success.

啊，世尊！我向「圆满正觉的那位」顶礼——你以胜解安住于法中，以真实告诉了我。

万祇沙向佛陀表达由衷的感谢与礼敬。「圆满正觉」（sammāsambodhi，正等觉）是佛陀证悟的标准称号。「你以真实告诉了我」：这不是客套话，而是对佛陀如实宣说（yathābhūtaṃ）的赞叹——佛陀不安慰、不回避，以真实情况回答了万祇沙的问题。对于一位正在哀悼导师的弟子，这种如实的确认——「是的，他解脱了」——是最深的安慰，也是最可靠的依止。

Vaṅgīsa: Blessed One! I bow to the Perfectly Enlightened One — you, abiding in the Dhamma with right understanding, have told me truly.

The gratitude is not formulaic. Vaṅgīsa has asked a question about someone he loved and revered — and has received a direct, unambiguous answer. 'You have told me truly' (saccaṃ akkhāsi) is a recognition of the Buddha's defining quality: he does not say what comforts, nor what he thinks the questioner wants to hear, but what is actually the case. For a grieving student, this truthfulness — 'yes, he has passed beyond' — is both the deepest comfort and the most reliable foundation for continued practice. It confirms that the path leads where it is said to lead.

总结 · Summary

万祇沙经是经集中最具个人情感色彩的经典之一。诗人比丘万祇沙以诗偈向佛陀询问已故导师尼拘律是否已经解脱——这是一个悲伤的弟子对所爱之师的真切关怀，也是对修行究竟有效与否的深层追问。佛陀以「他已断除渴爱、渡越苦海」作出明确的肯定。万祇沙随即以诗赞叹导师的慧眼——看见诸行生灭、以此解脱。经文以佛陀的再次确认（「是的，他已圆满解脱」）结束。这首经是一首哀歌，也是一份确认：修行的路是真实的，终点是真实的，那些真正走到终点的人，已真正超越了死亡。

The Vaṅgīsa Sutta is one of the most personally affecting texts in the Sutta Nipata. The poet-monk Vaṅgīsa — grieving his teacher Nigrodhakappa — asks the Buddha directly: did he attain liberation? The question is both personal (the student's love for his teacher) and doctrinal (does the practice actually work?). The Buddha's confirmation — craving cut, the flood crossed — is unambiguous. Vaṅgīsa responds with praise of his teacher's 'eye of wisdom' that saw the arising and passing of all phenomena. The Buddha closes with a second confirmation. The sutta is simultaneously an elegy for a beloved teacher and a declaration of confidence in the path: those who walk it truly do arrive; death is genuinely crossed; the promise is kept.
