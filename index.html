Sutta Nipata

经集

巴利藏 · 小部 · Bhikkhu Bodhi 英译

The Sutta Nipata (经集) is among the oldest texts in the Pali Canon, preserved in the Khuddaka Nikāya. Its 71 suttas span five chapters (vaggas), mixing verse and prose on the themes of ethics, renunciation, non-clinging, and the path to liberation. The Aṭṭhakavagga and Pārāyanavagga are regarded as the most ancient strata of the collection. The beloved Metta Sutta — the Buddha's teaching on boundless loving-kindness toward all beings — is found here in the first vagga. Mettañca sabbalokasmi — loving-kindness toward the whole world.
